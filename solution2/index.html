Chicken

Chickens are subspecies of the red junglefowl, a type of domesticated fowl, originally from Asia. Rooster or cock is a term for an adult male bird. A younger bird may be called a cockerel; a male that has been castrated is a capon. The adult female bird is called a hen.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle, particularly skeletal muscle. Humans have been eating beef since prehistoric times. Beef is a source of protein and nutrients. Most beef can be used as is by merely cutting into certain parts, such as roasts.

Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, accompanying a variety of ingredients such as seafood, often raw, and vegetables.
